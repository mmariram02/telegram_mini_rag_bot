# Data Science FAQ (Sample Doc)

Q: What is data science?
A: Data science is an interdisciplinary field that uses statistics, programming,
and domain knowledge to extract insights and value from data.

Q: What does a data scientist do?
A: A data scientist cleans data, explores patterns, builds models, and communicates
results to business stakeholders through dashboards, presentations, and reports.

Q: What skills are important?
A: Python, SQL, statistics, machine learning, and communication are key skills.
